description=[b]Smart Hutch adds automation and quality-of-life features for your chicken hutch:[/b]
description=
description=[b]Auto Close[/b]: Closes the hutch when it gets dirty and releases chickens to prevent sickness.  
description=[b]Auto Clean[/b]: Cleans the hutch automatically when the door is closed, then reopens once finished.  
description=[b]Auto Heal[/b]: Heals chickens inside the hutch with customizable health levels.  
description=[b]Passive Clean[/b]: Provides a steady reduction to dirtiness for balance.  
description=[b]Timed Door[/b]: Opens and closes the hutch at configurable hours.  
description=
description=All features and thresholds are fully customizable in both [i]Mod Settings[/i] and [i]Sandbox Options[/i].
