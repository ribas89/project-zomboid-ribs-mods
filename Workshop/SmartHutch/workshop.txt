description=Smart Hutch adds automation and quality-of-life features for your chicken hutch:
description=
description=- Auto Close: Closes the hutch when it gets dirty and releases chickens so they don't get sick.
description=- Auto Clean: Automatically cleans the hutch when the door is closed and opens it once cleaning is complete.
description=- Auto Heal: Heals chickens inside the hutch with customizable health levels.
description=- Passive Clean: Adds a passive reduction to dirtiness for balance.
description=- Timed Door: Opens and closes the hutch automatically at configurable hours.
description=
description=All features and thresholds can be customized in both the mod settings and sandbox options.
